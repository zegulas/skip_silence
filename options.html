Auto-2× on Silence – Settings

How it works: The extension monitors audio levels in videos and speeds up playback during silent moments (like when a professor is writing on a board).

Note: You may need to click on the video or page first for audio analysis to work due to browser security policies.

Troubleshooting

  • If the extension isn't working, try clicking on the video first
  • Check the browser console (F12) for error messages
  • Some sites may block audio analysis for security reasons
  • Lower the silence threshold if it's not detecting quiet moments
  • Increase the grace period if it's switching speeds too quickly
